THE RULES
OF THE ROAD.

01
Mandatory 25° Controlled Oversteer

Every marked corner within Drift City requires a minimum 25° controlled drift. Exiting corners with all four wheels tracking straight is treated as a traffic infraction. ASN-9 laser arrays verify angle at every marked intersection.

▶ $400 AUTOMATED CITATION — NO EXCEPTIONS
02
Green Wave Compliance

Arterial roads require sustained speeds of 70–110 km/h to remain in the synchronized green wave managed by ASN-9. Dropping below or exceeding the window disrupts the flow cascade for all downstream intersections.

▶ $250 FLOW DISRUPTION FINE
03
No Stopping in Transit Lanes

Transit lanes operate as perpetual-flow corridors. Any vehicle that decelerates below 40 km/h outside a designated zone is flagged as a hazard. Emergency stop bays are located every 1.2 km on all major arterials.

▶ $500 LANE STAGNATION FINE
04
Race License Mandatory

Residency requires a minimum FIA-recognized regional racing license or an equivalent Drift City Qualification Certificate (DCQC). Licenses are re-evaluated bi-annually. Any lapse in standing triggers automatic residency review.

05
Monthly Tire Compliance Inspection

All vehicles must present for mandatory tire inspection at licensed Drift City service bays every 30 days. Worn tyres below 2mm tread depth receive an automatic impound order. Replacements must use DC-approved compound grades.

▶ $800 UNSAFE TIRE FINE + IMPOUND

Drift City residential code prohibits conventional front-door pedestrian entry. All home access is through the garage bay. The garage is the ceremonial and functional primary entrance — the living room, the statement, the identity.

30-Car Gallery Standard

Every Drift City residence must feature a minimum 30-vehicle gallery-grade garage. Climate control, hydraulic lift systems, and gallery lighting are code requirements. Vehicles on display must be operational — static collections without valid registration are prohibited.
