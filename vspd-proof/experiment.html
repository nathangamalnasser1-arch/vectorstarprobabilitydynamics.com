How to read the visualization

Standard mode: Each particle is drawn as a circle. Size and brightness represent probability density (derived from energy and local particle count). Brighter/larger = higher probability.

VSPD mode: The same data is converted into a vector field. Arrows show the direction and magnitude of probability flow (from momentum and density gradient). The field evolves deterministically with event_index.
